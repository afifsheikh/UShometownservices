    A Healthy Home is a Happy Home

    Every time your HVAC system runs, all sorts of contaminants are pulled into the duct system. This build-up of dirt, dust, pollen, and other tiny particles can cause your home’s duct system to become a breeding ground for fungi, bacteria, mold, and other harmful microbes.

    Our home duct cleaning service can remove these contaminants from your duct system, improving the quality of air in your home. But that’s not all; duct cleaning can also increase the efficiency of your home’s heating and cooling systems, and significantly reduce your energy bills as a result.

    How Home Duct Cleaning Works

    At United Home Services, our home duct cleaning experts use an advanced source removal system: the HEPA-AIRE method. This system is considered the gold standard in home duct cleaning technology, and is used by top professionals across the country.

    The HEPA-AIRE system is comprised of two key components:

    The power compressor and vacuum allow our technicians to reach the farthest nooks and crannies in the duct system with minimal noise.

    Why Get Your Ducts Cleaned?

    Considering the current COVID-19 pandemic, improving your indoor air quality is one of the best ways you can stay protected. And getting your home’s ductwork deep cleaned will help you achieve that.

    Just consider the following:

    • Do you have a smoker in your house?
    • Does anyone in your home suffer from nasal congestion, headaches, or sinus-related problems at night or after waking up?
    • Does anyone in your home have asthma, allergies, or other respiratory issues?
    • Do you notice stale or musty odors when your air conditioner or furnace is running?
    • Do you have pets that are always shedding?
    • Do you feel like there is not enough airflow coming from your air vents?
    • Do you notice a thin layer of dust on your furniture soon after cleaning?

    If you answered “yes” to any of these questions, it most likely means that the air quality in your home is bad. It could mean that your home’s ducts have become full of contaminants that are being circulated throughout your home every time you run the AC.

    Ideally, ducts should be cleaned at least every 3-5 years, depending on factors like the level of pollution in your area. Similarly, if you live somewhere with a high count of pollen (like Connecticut and Pennsylvania), you should get your ducts cleaned professionally at least once every 2 years. 

    Increase Your HVAC’s Efficiency and Save Money

    As airborne contaminants accumulate within the air duct system, the efficiency of the air conditioning and heating system also diminishes. These pollutants trapped within the ductwork will keep circulating throughout your home, and eventually start impeding the air flow.

    As air flow starts dwindling, the ability of your AC or furnace to run efficiently is affected. Clogged air ducts cause the AC and furnace to work harder than normal and use more energy to cool and heat your home.

    If you can’t tell why you’re suddenly getting higher energy bills, your AC and furnace could be the culprits. Our home duct cleaning services can help improve the quality of air inside your home and enhance the efficiency of your HVAC system.
